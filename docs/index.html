Everything You Need

Complete control over your Lenovo laptop with an intuitive interface

Power Profiles

Quiet, Balanced, Performance modes with custom TDP control. Seamless Fn+Q integration.

Fan Control

Real-time fan RPM monitoring with smooth animations. Auto and Full Speed modes.

Battery Management

Conservation mode, rapid charge, and detailed battery health monitoring.

GPU Switching

Switch between Hybrid, NVIDIA, and Integrated graphics with a single click.

RGB Keyboard

Full RGB control with static, breath, wave, and hue effects via LegionAura.

CPU/GPU Overclock

Control CPU frequency, TDP limits, and GPU core/memory offsets.

Temperature Monitoring

Real-time CPU, GPU, and IC temperature with detailed hardware stats.

Display Controls

G-Sync toggle, brightness backlight, display overdrive, and refresh rate selection.
